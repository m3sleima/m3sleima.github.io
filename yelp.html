Yelp Food Truck Locator

because finding a food truck should not depend on luck...

CONCEPT DESIGN  |  UI/UX DESIGN  |  IOS PROTOTYPING


CONTEXT

During the summer, you'll often find food trucks driving around in search of a good, convenient spot to set up. This makes it very hard for people to track the trucks' locations throughout the day. You have to be there at the right time and place in order to enjoy some greasy goodness. In hopes of solving this issue, I mocked up a possible Yelp feature that will help users quickly locate and discover food trucks around the city.

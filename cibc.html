CIBC Hello Home App Icon Redesign

ICON DESIGN  |  BRANDING  |  PROTOTYPING


CONTEXT

CIBC recently launched a mortgage mobile application on IOS in hopes of improving the overall customer experience while applying to a mortgage. I was really inspired by the concept of the app and wanted to redesign their app icon into something that would scream "CIBC". Please note that this is a personal project inspired by CIBC's logo. This design is in no way affiliated with CIBC.

Print a Party

ENTREPRENEURSHIP  |  GRAPHIC DESIGN  |  PRINTING  |  CUSTOMER SERVICE


Back in 2014, I founded Print a Party; an online store that offers cutesy, custom-made and easy to use party supplies for various occasions! My designs are extremely simple, yet stunningly elegant.

Through working closely with my customers, I was able to design compelling stationery to fit their needs. My goal was to create unique, cheap party supplies that will stand out... You name it, I designed it!

Through this personal project and working closely with my customers, I was able to pursue my passion for business and my deep interest in making people happy. In 2016, after making over 500 sales in the span of 16 months, I decided to hault Print a Party's progress to focus my efforts on some of my other passions. This was a great experience overall and I am truly honoured and greatful to have shared many of my clients' happiest moments.
